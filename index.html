About

Craig Garrigan was born and resides in Dundee where he works as a software engineer for the NCR Corporation. He currently works on the NCR Interactive Banker server team.

In 2013, Craig graduated with a First Class Honours degree in Computer Science from the University of St Andrews. He was also a finalist in the ScotlandIS Young Software Engineer of the Year Awards 2013 for his Honours project, "Gesture-based Whiteboard Capture using the Microsoft Kinect". After four years spent in academia, Craig has lost the ability to write in first person.
